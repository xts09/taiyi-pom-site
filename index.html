Selection logic

From part requirement to material direction

For B2B material evaluation, the most important work is not simply choosing a product name. We first clarify the molded part, then translate working conditions into a practical POM material direction.

1

Application

Part type, working condition, movement mode, and assembly environment.

2

Performance targets

Wear, friction, stiffness, flow, dimensional stability, and color needs.

3

Material direction

Natural POM Resin, Low-friction POM Compound, Wear-resistant POM Compound, Glass Fiber Reinforced POM Compound, or Conductive POM Compound.

4

Evaluation support

Grade review, document support, sample discussion, and batch communication.
